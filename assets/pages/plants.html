Plant Like Organisms

If you travel into deep space and look back at Raviano through a sophisticated telescope, you could measure what’s called the vegetation red edge (VRE). The vegetation red edge is a mixture of red and infrared light that is reflected by plants on Raviano’s surface. Because of clouds, ice masses, and large oceans, the vegetation red edge on Raviano is actually fairly small and difficult to measure. If you went millions of years back in history, it would be even harder to see. Today’s terrestrial plants give off a more red and infrared light than the mosses and ferns that evolved earlier in Raviano’s history.
